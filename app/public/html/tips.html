Quick Tips

Meditate and Excercise
Meditating and exercising are a great way to keep your body healthy, and several studies say they help with studying as well!
Study everyday
To make sure you don't have an information overload, try to study your notes a little bit whenever you're free!
Use class time wisely
To maximize what you learn, you should be taking notes, reading lectures before hand, and creating flash cards!
Get into a routine
Getting into a routine lets you get used to studying, and helps make the transition to post-secondary much easier!
